Alicante Airport to Alicante – Quick Bus C6 Guide

URL: https://alicanteabout.com/transport/from-alicante-airport-to-alicante-city-centre-by-bus/

There are several ways to go from Alicante airport to Alicante city centre and the cheapest is by bus. It is a quick and reliable service. After reading this article you will have a better idea of how to go by bus using the regular Alicante Airport shuttle bus.
Bus Line C6. Alicante-Airport
The C-6 bus connects Alicante Airport to the city centre in about 30 minutes , departing from the airport’s 2nd floor . Operating 24/7 , it runs every 20 minutes from 6:20 am to 10:30 pm and hourly overnight . A single fare is 3.85€ and a bundle card is 6€ for 10 trips. Card and cash accepted.
The bus line between Alicante Airport and Alicante is bus line C-6 and its colour is blue (here more about Alicante’s bus system and its colours) . Some important stops are Alicante’s Coach Station , Alicante Postiguet Beach , Luceros TRAM and Alicante’s Train Station .

Bus C6 Stop Location at Alicante Airport
Alicante Airport bus C6 stops on the 2nd floor of Alicante Airport. Note that all buses stop at level 2. This is the top level at the airport, the same as departures. There are lifts right outside the arrivals door that are very convenient to get to the bus stop. There are also mechanical ramps or traditional stairs to use.
Bus C6 waiting for passengers at Alicante Airport
C6 Bus Prices to Alicante or Alicante Airport
The price for one single trip to or from Alicante airport is 3.85€ wherever you get in or off. Card and cash payments are permitted. Contactless, chip and magnetic debit or credit cards are accepted. Buy the tickets and bundles to the bus driver inside the actual bus. Note that your bank might charge you for euro currency payments .
For frequent travellers to the airport, there is also available a bundle of 10 trips for 12€ but 50% discounted until 31-01-2024 to a final price of 6€. The chip travel card cost 2€ . To buy the travel card ask the driver on the bus, this is the only place to buy and recharge the bundle for the airport bus line C6.

Temporary Travel Card Bundle Discounts
The Spanish government and Alciante’s city hall created a temporary public transport discount to help citizens tackle the increase in living prices.
Until 31-01-2024, there is a temporary discount of 50% when buying the 10 trips travel card bundle, which costs 6€ until the temporary discount end date.
C6 Alicante Airport Bus Stops
Some of the bus stops in Alicante are: Plaza de los Luceros , Alfonso el Sabio and the train station . All those are very convenient locations as you can commute to Renfe’s trains , Tram , the European EUIPO office or other bus services very easily.

F.A.Q.
How much is the C6 Bus from Alicante Airport to Alicante?
A one-way trip costs 3.85€. A 10-trip bundle card costs 12€ but is reduced to 6€ until 31-1-2024. Only contactless and bundle cards are accepted.
How far is Alicante from Alicante Airport?
The distance between Alicante and Alicante Airport is 11km (7.8 mi). The journey takes about 15 minutes by car and from 20 to 30 min by bus.
How to go from Alicante airport to Alicante?
Take the bus C6. It stops on floor 2 at the airport and in Alicante city centre (departures floor). Another option is to take a taxi or pre-book the ride with a transfer company.
