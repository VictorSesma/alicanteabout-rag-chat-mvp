Alicante Airport is the 5th busiest in Spain. All the passengers need to get by car, coach or other road-based public transport. Let’s find out how to use the bus services.
Alicante Airport Coach and Bus Station
Does Alicante Airport have bus services? Yes. You can use bus or coach services to go to Alicante, Benidorm, Elche and most of the other cities in Costa Blanca and Alicante Province from Alicante-Elche Airport. Use bus C6 for Alicante city centre .
Here you can find the ultimate resource with all the routes, schedules and links to buy the tickets.

Where to Find the Coach/Bus Stops and Tickets
The airport has a bus station at the top level 2. Use the lifts or mechanical ramps to get into level 2 from arrivals, departures or car parking.
Alicante Airport to Alicante City
The bus travelling from Alicante Airport to Alicante City Centre is line C-6 . The price is 3.85€. You can pay only with a debit or credit card using the chip, contactless or magnetic card band.
C6 Bus at Alicante Airport
Ticket price: 3.85€
Timetable: 5 am to 12 pm. Every 20 min
Buy the tickets : At the bus. This is the official website .
The bus stops at the train station , TRAM station, city centre and nearby popular hotels. The ride is about 20 minutes long, depending on the traffic.
Alicante Airport to Benidorm
The route from Alicante Airport to Benidorm is operated by Alsa. They are the biggest couch operator in Spain using comfortable and safe buses. The coaches usually feature WiFi and toilets.
Ticket price: From less than 10€
Timetable: The first service is at 6:15 am. The last service is at 11 pm. One bus every hour. (Note this timetable might change by the time you are reading this, check the website for the most updated timetable)
Buy the tickets : Alsa website using a credit/debit card or at the bus using only cash. Download their app for Android or iPhone .
Alicante Airport Coach Stop to Benidorm and Alicante
There are two stops at Benidorm: Avenida Europa and Benidorm Coach Station so it is recommended which one is nearer to your destination.

Other Routes and Tickets
There are other routes available from Alicante-Elche Airport. The easiest is to go to Alsa’s website and check if your destination is there. Remember that the Airport-Alicante centre line C-6 has a stop at Alicante’s bus station which gives you more destination options.
If you are looking for a route that is not on the list feel free to tell us in the comments and we will add it to the list for you.
Where is the Bus Stop at the Airport?
Alicante Airport Coach Stop on the Second Floor
It is situated at the top level 2 at Alicante Airport. Lifts and elevators are available.
Alternative Transport from Alicante Airport
Alicante-Elche airport is connected by road only. There are no railway connections available to Alicante Airport at the moment but some studies have started on how to connect the airport with nearby cities such as Alicante and Benidorm.
There are several alternative means of transport you can use apart from the bus to reach your destination.
Alicante-Elche Airport has a taxi stop to get rides to other cities such as Benidorm, Torrevieja or Altea.
There is a free fast parking area where private cars can pick up or drop off passengers. Transfers companies can operate on it as well.
There are several car rental companies with pick-up and drop-off services at the airport.
There are also long-term parking services that can hold your private car during the time of your trip duration.
Where is Alicante’s airport coach station?
It is situated at top level 2
Go do I go to Alicante from the airport?
Use the airport bus line C6 . The fee is 3.85€ to pay using a contactless card or cash.
